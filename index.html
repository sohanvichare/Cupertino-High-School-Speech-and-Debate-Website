Cupertino High School Speech and Debate



Cupertino Speech and Debate is an educational organization committed to providing high school students the opportunity and environment to develop and hone speaking, argumentation, and presentation skills. Under the leadership of Cupertino High math teacher Marty De and the support of an incredibly hardworking officer team, Tino SD has found huge amounts of success in the few years that it has existed. Members have been crowned tournament champions on multiple occasions and have reached national ranks within the SD community. More importantly, Tino SD strives to ensure each and every member gets the most out their time on the team. From teaching basic speaking skills and helping develop arguments to providing a platform for expression and allowing students to build crucial self confidence, Cupertino Speech and Debate truly fosters growth and development in all of its amazing members.
